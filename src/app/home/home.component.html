About Us

Rotary is a global network of 1.2 million supportive decision-makers who see a world where people come together and take action to bring about lasting change - in the world, in their communities and in themselves.

WE WORK DIFFERENTLY

We see things in an unexpected way: our multidisciplinary approach enables us to approach troubles with creativity.
We think in an unexpected way: we put our authority and aptitude at the administration of societal causes and we find imaginative arrangements.
We are capable: our enthusiasm and constancy produce enduring change.
We have an effect in our city and on the planet: our individuals are available everywhere throughout the world.

What we do

Rotary members trust that we should all make a move to solve the world's most squeezing issues.
Our in excess of 35,000 clubs cooperate to:
Promote peace.
Fight diseases.
Bring clean water, sanitation and hygiene.
To look after mothers and their children.
Support education.
Develop local economies.

Our Mission

We serve others, and we promote integrity, mutual understanding, goodwill and peace through our network of professionals and leaders.
